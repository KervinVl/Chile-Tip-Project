The average price of a 5-day trip for a family of 4 to Chile is $3,508. Depending on activities,
budgeting $29 to $60 per person per day for transportation and enjoying local restaurants is
recommended. Daily expenses per day for 4 people ranges between $116 to $240 only including
Food, Travel, and Sightseeing. 2 recognized hotels in Santiago Chile are Sheraton Santiago Hotel
and Convention Center and Mercure Santiago Centro.

Sheraton Santiago Hotel

Sheraton Santiago Hotel is 5-Star Sophisticated hotel with stately rooms, 2 restaurants, a full-service
spa & a bar with a pisco menu. The price per night for a family of 4 in Sheraton Santiago
Hotel is $1485 for 5 nights.

Mercure Santiago Centro

The Mercure Santiago Centro is a 4-Star hotel with modern rooms that has everything you need.
The price per night for a family of 4 in Mercure Santiago Centro is $526 for 5 nights.

What is the best option?

If the highest quality of service is wanted Sheraton Santiago Hotel is the way to go, but if a more
affordable hotel with still good quality service is wanted Mercure Santiago Centro may be your
choice.
