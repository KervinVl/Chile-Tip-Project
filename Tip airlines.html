The two major airlines in Chile are Avianca and LAN Airlines.

Avianca

The average Avianca ticket price to fly from Miami to Santiago, Chile is $490 round trip.
Avianca average duration of flight is 8 hours nonstop from Miami to Santiago. In Avianca food
service is offered, meals and beverages will be individually pre-packaged. In Avianca there is a
flight entertainment system that will only be available on flights longer than 3 hours.

LAN Airlines

The average LAN airlines ticket price to fly from Miami to Santiago, Chile is $533 round trip.
LAN Airlines' average duration of flight is 8 hours nonstop from Miami to Santiago. In LAN
Airlines On flights longer than 7 hours, there is an award-winning menu with three options of
dishes inspired by international and local cuisine concepts, as well as a vegetarian option. LAN
Airlines have the latest movie releases, the best TV hits, thousands of songs, interactive maps
and much more on individual screens up to 12 inches.
