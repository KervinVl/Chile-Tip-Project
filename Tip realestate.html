The Chilean legislation allows a foreigner to buy real estate, whether he is resident or not. A
passport number is enough to buy a property. The average price of a home in a middle-class
neighborhood in Santiago with three bedrooms and two bathrooms is about $255,000 and
$170,000 in the rest of the country. Chile house prices grew 11.7% YoY in Sep 2021, following
an increase of 12.8% YoY in the previous month. YoY growth data is updated monthly,
available from Jan 2005 to Sep 2021, with an average growth rate of 4.9%. House price data
reached an all-time high of 12.8% in Aug 2021 and a record low of -3.0% in Dec 2016.
