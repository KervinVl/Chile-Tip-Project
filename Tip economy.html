Chile is the world's leading producer of copper, and growth in GDP is driven by exports of
minerals, wood, fruit, seafood, and wine. Chile's economic freedom score is 74.4, making its
economy the 20th freest in the 2022 Index. Chile is ranked 2nd among 32 countries in the
Americas region, and its overall score is above the regional and world averages.
